Exercises:

1. How many € you must give to get 2750 $ at and exchange rate of 1.63?

2. How many € you must pay to get 700 $ at and exchange rate of 1.08?

3. How many € you must give to get 2100 $ at and exchange rate of 1.23?

4. How many € you must pay to get 1200 $ at and exchange rate of 1.87?

5. Find the € you need to buy 2450 $ at and exchange rate of 1.05.

Solutions:

1. You get 2750 $ for [1687.12 €] at an exchange rate of 1.63

2. You get 700 $ for [648.15 €] at an exchange rate of 1.08

3. You get 2100 $ for [1707.32 €] at an exchange rate of 1.23

4. You get 1200 $ for [641.71 €] at an exchange rate of 1.87

5. You get 2450 $ for [2333.33 €] at an exchange rate of 1.05
