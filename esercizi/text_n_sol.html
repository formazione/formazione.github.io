Exercises:

1. If you got 1600 $, how many euro you will get at 1.37 as exchange rate?

2. If you got 1300 $, how many euro you will get at 1.96 as exchange rate?

3. How many € you must give to get 500 $ at and exchange rate of 1.87?

4. How many € you must give to get 2500 $ at and exchange rate of 1.86?

5. Change 2950 $ in euro at an exchange rate of 1.38.

Solutions:

1. You get 1600 $ for [1167.88 €] at an exchange rate of 1.37

2. You get 1300 $ for [663.27 €] at an exchange rate of 1.96

3. You get 500 $ for [267.38 €] at an exchange rate of 1.87

4. You get 2500 $ for [1344.09 €] at an exchange rate of 1.86

5. You get 2950 $ for [2137.68 €] at an exchange rate of 1.38
