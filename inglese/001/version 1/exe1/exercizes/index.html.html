1. Mini-Storia: "Emma's First Day at University"

Emma was nervous about her first day at university. She had prepared her books the night before and set her alarm clock for 7 AM. When she woke up, the sun was shining brightly through her bedroom window.

After a quick breakfast, she grabbed her backpack and walked to the bus stop. The campus looked enormous when she arrived. Emma felt a bit overwhelmed by all the new faces around her. She approached a friendly-looking girl near the library.

"Excuse me, could you help me find the English Department?" Emma asked politely. The girl smiled and gave her clear directions. Emma thanked her and felt more confident about starting this new chapter of her life.

2. Domande di Comprensione

1. Why was Emma nervous on her first day?
2. What time did Emma set her alarm clock for?
3. How did Emma feel when she saw the campus?
4. Who did Emma ask for help?
5. What did Emma ask the girl to help her find?
