The marketing mix

In order to meet its business objectives, a company would develop and implement a strategic marketing plan, based on segmentation targeting and positioning.

In order to achieve the objectives of Strategic marketing, a company will device an operational marketing plan.

When it comes to launching a special offer a product or service the key to success lies in one simple recipe, the marketing mix: product, price, place, promotion.

These ingredients are more commonly known as the 4 P's. A clever mix with just the right blend of the 4 P's is the key to a good business strategy.

It must ensure consistency in all business efforts, demonstrate market adaptability and adjust the fitting with a company profile.

This classic recipe is often modified and expanded upon by introducing new parameters that take into account the consumer, but that of course makes for a whole new set of recipes.
